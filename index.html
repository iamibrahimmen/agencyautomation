// FAQ

Questions Answered

Do I need to know how to code or use any technical tools? ▼
Zero technical knowledge required. We handle the entire build, setup, and deployment. You get a live system with simple controls — if something breaks, you message us and we fix it. Your job is to run your business, not to learn automation tools.
How long does it take to go live? ▼
Most systems go live within 7–14 days. The Starter plan is typically 7 days, Growth plan takes up to 14 days because of the added complexity. Enterprise timelines depend on scope — we'll give you an exact timeline during the discovery call.
What tools do the automations connect to? ▼
We connect to WhatsApp, Gmail, Google Sheets, HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Shopify, Facebook/Instagram, Calendly, Notion, Slack, Stripe, and 400+ more platforms. If your tool has an API or webhook, we can automate it.
Do I own the automation system after it's built? ▼
Yes — 100%. You own all workflows, all credentials, and all data. We give you full access and documentation. The Growth plan includes ongoing optimization because most clients want us to keep improving the system, but you're never locked in.
What if the automation breaks or stops working? ▼
Starter clients get 30-day support. Growth clients get ongoing support as part of the monthly retainer. Enterprise clients get 24/7 SLA monitoring. Our systems are built for stability — but when something does go wrong, we fix it fast.
